Outreach

I believe it is important, necessary, and fun to engage with the general public, and share some outputs of our research.
In particular, I feel very strongly about the possibilities of the interaction between arts and fundamental science. Ask me about the black hole piano, and I'll talk for hours!
I am still a newbie in this field. Therefore, I hope the following list grows in the near future. If you have any cool ideas, please get in touch!

  • Together with Anna Brandenberger, Anna Knorr, Shawn Skelton, and Manu Srivastava, our pitch Simulating the Future through Science Fiction was awarded the winner of the PSI 15th Anniversary Reunion Pitch Competition.
  • I was the runner up of the Nordic Science Slam in March 2024. In the (in)famouts Studenterhuset I declaimed a ballad about the birth and death of a black hole.
